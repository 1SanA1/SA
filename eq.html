Cricket Equipment and Positions

There are many equipment in cricket. This is because the ball is really hard and the batsmen need protection. There are also many positions in cricket (e.g. batsman, bowler).

List of equipment

  • Stumps - three wooden upright pieces which form a wicket
  • Ball
  • Bat
  • Pads - a protective, long guard covring your knee and shin.
  • Helmet
  • Batting/Wicket Keeping Gloves
  • Abdominal Guard
  • Chest Guard
  • Elbow Guard
  • Bat Grips - It allows you to grip the bat easily

List of positions

  1. Batsman - A batsman is a person who is an expert in hitting the ball when the ball is bowled to them. They do not bowl in cricket and are usually up in the order.
  2. Wicket Keeper - A wicket keeper is the one who stands behind the stumps and the batsman. The wicket keeper is equiped with a helmet, gloves and pads.
  3. Bowlers - There are two types of bowlers in cricket:
    • Spinner - A bowler who is who is an expert at spinning the ball. They do not bowl really fast.
    • Seamer - A fast bowler.
  4. Fielder - a person who occupies a defensive postition while their opposition is batting.
  5. All Rounder - a person who is good at bowling and batting.
